Directors Message

On behalf of Rastriya Siksha Parisad, I thank you for showing your interest on this organization. The primary aim of this organization is to present various types of computer courses to all types of students, starting from students of urban areas to the students of rural areas. We have designed the courses as per industrial requirements of the employers. So that, when a student completes a course, they don’t just get a certificate with them, they carry a confidence to join the IT industry. We are encouraging the students to join our programs not for certificates, but for their future. 

We have structured our courses into various categories, with respect to the time needed for the completion of the course. We have vocational courses, that can help the students nurture their skills and become employed. We have not only improvised the education, but we have worked on the entire process. Starting from taking admission till getting the certificate. Each and every students will have their own profile with user id and password, so that they can track their progress any time. We have kept the certificate verification for the employers, to increase the trust among them.

I encourage the students to enroll in different courses, and also participate in the various events we organize. Because nothing is more important than the career of a student.

Best of luck.
